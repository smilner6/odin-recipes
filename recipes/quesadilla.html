Quesadilla

Description

Quesadillas are simple and fast.

It is easy to add healthy elements to this meal.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • 1 cup black beans
  • Diced onion
  • Diced green peppers
  • Shredded cheese
  • Hot sauce and sour cream

Steps

  1. Fry onion, peppers and black beans in oil over medium
  2. Set aside
  3. In clean pan, fry tortilla until crispy
  4. Load fillings into tortolla and sprinkle with cheese
  5. Serve with sauces, slice into triangles

Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Chicken noodle soup is simple and nourishing.

This is great to cook when you're sick.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked chopped chicken breast
  • 1 bag egg noodles
  • 3 cartons chicken broth
  • 3 large chopped carrots
  • 3 large chopped celery ribs
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • Thyme, salt, pepper, rosemary to taste

Steps

  1. Boil chiken broth in a large pot
  2. Add egg noodles, seasonings, and veggies, boil on medium for 15 minutes
  3. Add chicken and season to taste
  4. Serve warm
